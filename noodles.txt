Ingredients:
 - 2 cups of water
 - 2 packs of noodles
 - 2 table spoon of vegetable oil
 - Chilling pepper
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 Onion bud

Directions:
 1. Add 2 cups of water and 2 eggs into a pot, allow to boil for about 10 Minutes.

 2. Slice chilling pepper and onion bud to taste and add together with 2 table spoon
    of vegetable oil and noodles into the already boiling pot of water and egg.

 3. Allow to boiling for about 5 minutes more until water is dried.
